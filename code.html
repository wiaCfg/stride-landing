Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about StradeAI

Is it really free?

expand_more

Yes! The core features of StradeAI are completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to reliable nutrition information. We may introduce premium features in the future with advanced analytics and personalized coaching, but the essential tools will always remain free.

Which languages are supported?

expand_more

Currently, the bot supports English, Spanish, and French with high accuracy. We are actively working on adding more languages including German, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, and Chinese. Language expansion is a top priority for Q1 2026.

How accurate is the nutrition data?

expand_more

Our AI is trained on an extensive database of over 1 million food items and leverages data from verified nutritional sources including USDA and international food databases. We achieve 95%+ accuracy in most cases. While we strive for precision, the analysis should be considered a close estimate. For medical or competition purposes, always consult a registered dietitian.

Is my data private and secure?

expand_more

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All data is encrypted end-to-end using industry-standard protocols. Personal information is anonymized and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties or use it for advertising. You have full control and can delete your data at any time from the settings menu.

What is WADA compliance?

expand_more

WADA stands for the World Anti-Doping Agency. Our supplement verification feature cross-references ingredients against the official WADA Prohibited List to help athletes ensure their supplements don't contain banned substances. This is crucial for professional and competitive athletes who undergo drug testing. We update our database regularly to reflect the latest WADA regulations.

Can I use this for weight loss or muscle gain?

expand_more

Yes! StradeAI is perfect for any fitness goal. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, improve athletic performance, or simply maintain a healthy diet, our tracking and insights will help you stay on track. You can set custom goals and receive personalized recommendations based on your targets.
